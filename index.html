Hunza Adventures

An Exhilarating Tour

Hunza Adventures is offering different yet attractive tours of Hunza Valley and Khunjerab Pass (Pakistan-China Border). Hunza Valley tour takes you to the lush green mountainous valleys in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan. Beyond its iconic glaciers, fertile apricot farms, and turquoise lakes, the region is also rich in cultural heritage.
Hunza Adventures gives you the opportunity to explore the local rich culture and customs, taste the local food and see history come to life in front of your eyes. These elements will transform you from a tourist into a great traveler.
You will experience the Khunjerab Pass, also explore the Ancient Silk Route, Baltit and Altit Fort, Fairy Meadows, and the world-famous mountains Nanga Parbat and Rakaposhi. For many years, Hunza Adventures has been organizing tours for enthusiasts from around the world to the majestic Hunza Valley.

Tour Itinerary

You will be greeted by the team of Hunza Adventure Tours at Islamabad International airport then transfer along with your luggage to a centrally located hotel in Islamabad.
Distance: 30 km / Driving Hours: 01 hr / Altitude: 540m / Activities: Sightseeing.

In the morning after breakfast, we will drive to Chilas en route we will stop for photography, lunch break and toilet stop then further drive to Chilas via Babusar Pass arrival in Chilas check-in at the hotel. Overnight stay in Chilas.
Distance: 460 km / Driving Hours: 11 - 12 hrs / Altitude: 1265m / Activities: Sightseeing

Departure for Fairy Meadows, we will transfer to the mountain jeep at Raikot Bridge for further drive to Tatto village on the narrow Jeep track. Arrive in Tatto village and start trek to Fairy Meadows, Arrive at Fairy Meadows check-in at the hut and after a short rest we will do some short walking around; overnight stay in the local style wooden huts.
Distance:95 km / Altitude: 3300m / Walk:3 hrs / Grade: Easy / Activities: Hiking

We will drive to Karimabad Hunza sightseeing en route, stop at the junction point of the three greatest mountain ranges, lunch break at Rakaposhi viewpoint, drive to Karimabad Hunza, arrival in Karimabad Hunza, check in at hotel after a short rest then we will go for the sunset Safari at Eagles nest. Overnight stay in Karimabad Hunza.
Distance: 220 km / Driving Hours:06 hrs / Altitude: 2400m / Activities: Sightseeing

In the morning after breakfast, we will drive to Hopper Valley after lunch then we will drive to Attabad Lake boating in Attabad Lake then further drive to Gulmit arrival in Gulmit check-in at the hotel. Overnight stay in Gulmit Upper Hunza Valley.
Distance:85 km / Driving Hours:4 hrs / Altitude:2500m / Activities: sightseeing

We will drive to Hussaini Village and visit Hussaini suspension bridges, then we will drive up to Upper Borith walking up to the viewpoint of Passu white glacier then drive to Passu village after lunch then we will have a village visit after completing the visit then we will drive back to Gulmit. Overnight Stay in Gulmit Upper Hunza Valley.
Distance:85 km / Driving Hours:4 hrs / Altitude:2500m / Activities: sightseeing

Full-day excursions to Khunjerab pass (The border between Pakistan and China). This route is a beautiful drive, with views of the high mountains of the Karakoram & the distant Pamir mountain ranges. At Khunjerab, now declared a national park, one can see Marcopolo Sheep, Snow leopard, Himalayan Ibexes. In the evening return back to the hotel.
Distance:272 km / Driving Hours:7 hrs / Altitude:4733m / Activities: sightseeing

After breakfast drive to Gilgit en-route short stop at Rakaposhi viewpoint for photography then further drive to Gilgit. We will then take a hair raising drive across Asia's longest suspension bridge - 600 feet long and wide enough only for one car at a time arrival in Gilgit check-in at hotel. Overnight stay in Gilgit.
Distance:145 km / Driving Hours:03 hrs / Altitude:1500m / Activities: sightseeing.

In the morning after breakfast then we will drive to Naran Valley via Babusar Pass en-route stop for photography then further drive to Naran Valley arrival in Naran check in at hotel after a short rest then we will visit Naran local bazar. Overnight stay in Naran Valley.
Distance:280 km / Driving Hours:6/7 hrs / Altitude:2400m / Activities: sightseeing

In the morning after breakfast, we will drive to Islamabad en route we will visit the Taxila Museum then further drive to Islamabad arrival in Islamabad after lunch then we will visit Faisal Mosque, Lok Versa, Saidpur Village, and Daman Koh and Raja Bazar after farewell dinner party then we will drop you at the airport to catch the flight back home.
Distance:376 km / Driving Hours:4-5 hrs / Altitude:540m / Activities: sightseeing
